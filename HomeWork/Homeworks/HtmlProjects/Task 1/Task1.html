Maggie

S.No Ingredients quantity
1. Noodles 1 Packet
2. Maggie Masala 1 Packet
3. Water 200 ml
4. Oil 1 TableSpoon
5. Salt 1 Teaspoon

Preparation:

firstly, in pan heat 1 tsp oil add 1 cup water and get to a boil. add 1 tsp salt now bread 1 pack maggi noodles roughly and add 1 pack Maggie Masala. mix well and boil for 2 minutes or until the noodles are cooked well. Now enjoy your masaledar maggi.
